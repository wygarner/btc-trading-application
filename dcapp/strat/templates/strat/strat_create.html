How it works

Your strategy is backtested over ~6 weeks of midprices collected every ~10 seconds from Coinbase. Positions are entered using limit orders, and exited with take profit limit orders and stop market orders. Fees from bitmex are calculated into every trade: A 0.025% rebate for maker orders and a 0.075% fee for taker orders. This backtest does not simulate real market conditions; orders are placed at the current midprice and filled instantly. The backtest is not completely accurate and does not represent future market conditions. Set your paramaters and test a strategy. Please be patient, it takes a while...
